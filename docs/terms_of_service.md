## Google Sign-In and Calendar Publishing

The app requires Google sign-in. When granted permission, the app can list writable Google Calendars and create, update, or delete Residency Scheduler events in a selected calendar.

When a schedule is published, the app may delete prior app-generated events for the same schedule month and calendar, then insert the current schedule. The app identifies its own events using private event metadata. Users should still review selected calendars carefully before publishing or wiping events.

If a resident email is present, the app may add that resident as an attendee to the assigned Google Calendar event and request Google to send event notifications.
